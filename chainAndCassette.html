Chain Swap

Over time, chains do wear out and will affect our shifting. When the gears start skipping, it is usually down to a worn chain, cassette or both. The type of chain you will need depends on the amount of gears you have. To determine how many gears you got, count the sprockets on your rear wheel. The other detail to look out for is the Discipline: Road and Mountain Bikes have different needs in regards to the chain. So when ordering a chain, please tell us the Discipline that you need.

Cassette Swap

Over time, cassettes do wear out and will affect our shifting. When the gears start skipping, it is usually down to a worn chain, cassette or both. The type of cassette you will need depend on the amount of gears you have as well the brand you are using- Shimano or Sram. To determine how many gears you got, count the sprockets on your rear wheel. Like chains, Cassettes are different between Road and Mountainbikes. So please, if you contact us for this service please tell us the Discipline you need.
